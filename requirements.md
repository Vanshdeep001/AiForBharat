# Requirements Document

## Brief about the Idea

### The Problem

- Low financial literacy among farmers, workers, and small business owners
- Poor awareness of government schemes for farmers and businesses
- No state-wise scheme guidance based on user location
- Language and literacy barriers block digital services
- Limited access to jobs, customers, and business support

### Our Vision

- Provide a voice-based assistant supporting local dialects for illiterate and low-literacy users
- Match job demand with user skills and services
- Answer queries on banking, laws, rights, and government schemes
- Create a single platform for banking, rights, jobs, and support
- Show state-wise schemes and financial aid using user location

## How different is it from any of the other existing ideas?

- Generic chatbots with no local or state context
- Text-only interfaces that require reading and typing
- Separate apps for banking, schemes, jobs, and rights
- Designed mainly for urban and educated users

## How will it be able to solve the problem?

- Voice-First Interaction, designed for illiterate and low-literacy users
- Role-Based Guidance, tailored for farmers, workers, and small business owners
- Use location intelligence to show only relevant state and local schemes
- Multilingual Support, works in local languages and dialects
- Unified Services, banking, rights, schemes, and jobs in one platform
- Act as a marketplace connector by matching jobs with user skills

## USP of the proposed solution

One voice. One app. All your rights, schemes, job opportunities, and small business support in your language.
